## Post-Vaccination Information

### Side Effects (Expected Within 5-14 Days)

- Mild fever (37.5°C - 38.5°C)
- Headache
- Muscle pain
- Fatigue
- Local site reaction (redness, swelling)

**Note:** These effects are temporary and indicate the vaccine is working.

### Serious Adverse Events (Rare)

Seek immediate medical attention if experiencing:
- High fever (>39°C)
- Severe allergic reaction
- Jaundice
- Neurological symptoms
