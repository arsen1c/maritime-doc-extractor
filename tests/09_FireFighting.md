## Important Notes

### Maintaining Competency

While this certificate is valid for 5 years, seafarers are encouraged to:
1. Participate in annual onboard fire drills
2. Maintain familiarity with vessel-specific fire systems
3. Attend refresher training annually (recommended)
4. Review ship emergency procedures regularly
5. Participate in tabletop exercises and simulations

### Revalidation Process

Revalidation can be achieved through:
1. **Attendance at certified refresher course** (2-3 days)
2. **Continuation of satisfactory maritime service** (if on active duty >80% of time on vessels with fire safety responsibilities)
3. **Case study assessment** (demonstrating practical knowledge from actual incidents)
