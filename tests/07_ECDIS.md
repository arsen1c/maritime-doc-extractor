## Important Notes

### Vessel Operating Requirements

1. **Type A ECDIS Requirement:** All vessels over 3000 GT operating internationally must have functioning Type A or Type B ECDIS
2. **Alternative Display:** Vessels may operate with type-rated alternative bridge display systems if ECDIS is non-functional
3. **Officers on Watch:** At least one qualified ECDIS-trained officer must be on watch at all times when operating in waters requiring ECDIS

### Renewal & Revalidation

- This endorsement is valid for **5 years** from date of issue
- Revalidation training (5-day refresher) must be initiated **6 months before expiry**
- Continuous maritime service maintains competency; no revalidation required if on active duty with ECDIS experience
- Masters with lapsed endorsements may undergo abbreviated revalidation training
