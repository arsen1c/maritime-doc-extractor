## Important Operational Requirements

### Mandatory During Tanker Service

1. **Oil Record Book:** Complete and maintain accurate OIL record book per MARPOL
2. **Vessel-Specific Training:** Complete familiarization with specific tanker's systems before commencing duties
3. **Competency Maintenance:** Maintain awareness through refresher training (recommended annually)
4. **Environmental Compliance:** Full compliance with MARPOL and environmental regulations
5. **Safety Protocols:** Strict adherence to hot work and hazardous area procedures

### Revalidation Options

- **Option 1:** Continuous service on oil tankers (maintains competency)
- **Option 2:** Attend 5-day refresher training course
- **Option 3:** Certification assessment with written exam and simulator exercise
